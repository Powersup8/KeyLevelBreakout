### Analysis

**What is a gap breakout?**
At 9:35, the first complete 5m bar closes. If price gapped UP at the open and
immediately carried through a key level during that first 5m candle, the BRK fires
at 9:35. The trader has no prior price action at that level — just a gap.

**Key behavioral differences of 9:35 signals:**
- High volume is almost certain (market open = peak volume)
- The breakout candle has no prior failed attempts at the level
- Gap-driven breakouts tend to fill — 'fade the gap' is a well-known strategy
- The opening bar often embeds multiple competing forces (gap fill, momentum, news)

**What the CONF rate tells us:**
CONF fires 5 bars (5m) after the BRK. A high CONF rate at 9:35 means price
continued in the breakout direction for ~5m. A low CONF rate means reversal.
