## CRITICAL FINDINGS (ranked by P&L impact)

### 1. EMA Hard Gate = +65% P&L improvement
- Non-EMA signals are NET NEGATIVE everywhere: pre-9:50 (-32.3 ATR), post-9:50 (-13.3 ATR)
- EMA gate all-day: 1841→1350 signals, +70.3→+115.9 ATR
- **Implement:** Suppress non-EMA after 9:50, dim before 9:50
- Source: `debug/v29-mc-rounds678.md` Round 6

### 2. Regime Score (EMA + VWAP) = real-time trend detector
- Both match signal direction (Score 2): **56.8% win, +110.3 ATR**
- Only one matches (Score 1): **37.7% win, -42.2 ATR** — actively harmful
- The "bear edge" in our data is NOT structural — it's regime:
  - UP days: bull 61.6% win, bear 34.4%
  - DOWN days: bear 60.4% win, bull 40.7%
- **EMA + VWAP detect regime in real-time** — no need for hindsight
- **Implement:** Use regime score in signal display (promote score=2, dim score=1)

### 3. Kill MC = removes complexity, ~0 P&L impact
- MC fires 99.4% before 9:50 on opening noise
- Opposing pairs (bull+bear) = coin flip
- Only 7 MC signals would fire after 9:50
- **Implement:** Remove MC signal generation, auto-confirm, orange labels, alerts
- Source: `debug/_session-context-mc-rethink.md`

### 4. Auto-Confirm R1 (EMA + Time<10:30) = +3.8 ATR, faster entry
- N=389, +0.106/signal, 58.6% win, MFE/MAE 1.60
- 12/13 symbols positive
- Replaces MC's two-step process
- **Implement:** EMA aligned + time < 10:30 → CONF pass without follow-through
- Source: `debug/v29-mc-rounds678.md` Round 7

### 5. CONF Pass = strongest quality signal (77.1% win, 0 disasters)
- ✓★: 88.6% win (N=44), ✓: 74.3% win (N=187)

### 6. Bull signals are near-zero edge overall (+0.00 P&L)

- Problem is we can't know the day's direction in advance
- **Solution:** Regime Score — bull + EMA + VWAP aligned = 58.8% win (good!)

### Key Insight: Two Separate Problems

**Problem 1: Morning signals we fire but don't confirm (289 ATR)**
- We fire the RIGHT signal at the RIGHT time but CONF kills it
- Directly recoverable with CONF extension or auto-confirm
- This is the v3.0 auto-confirm R1 change

**Problem 2: Midday/afternoon moves we never see (1,095+ ATR) — RESEARCHED**
- No levels to break → no signals generated
- This is a DESIGN LIMITATION, not a bug
- **Midday Treasure Hunt completed** (see `debug/midday-treasure-hunt.md`):
  - 43% of missed moves have a computable level within 0.15 ATR
  - Top 3 new levels (all backtest-positive):
    1. **PD_LAST_HR_L** — 55.6% win, +0.238/trade, +53.5 ATR (prior day last-hour low)
    2. **PD_MID** — 55.1% win, +0.209/trade, +34.9 ATR (prior day midrange)
    3. **VWAP_LOWER** — 53.3% win, +0.133/trade, +77.3 ATR (VWAP lower band)
  - **TRAPS:** 1HR_H (-62 ATR), VWAP_UPPER (-82 ATR), HALFDAY_L (-44 ATR) — DO NOT implement
  - Regime=2 filter NOT helpful (EMA already embedded in direction logic)
  - 32% of moves have NO level within 0.3 ATR — genuine "level desert"
  - Estimated recoverable: **~165 ATR from top 3 levels**
